Land, biodynamics & organics

New Zealand's most experienced family owned winery

Making wines true to our sites and inviting guests to enjoy them on our vineyards could never happen without our strong team. We’re privileged to find ourselves surrounded by such enthusiastic and talented people who appreciate our land, vines and wines. We’re learning from each other every day and grateful for what everybody brings to our vineyards and spaces.

Our story

You don't need to visit our place

To experience it

We’re keen on the truth. Our wines are grown with intense enthusiasm and detail in our vineyards, then made simply and naturally in our cellar. We’re pursuing pure and clear expressions of each of our three sites. Organic grapes are handpicked, fermented with vineyard derived yeasts, not subject to additives or fining, just minimal sulphur at bottling (sometimes none), and no filtration. All certified organic and benefitting from thoughtful insights using biodynamic practices. Here are our current release wines.
